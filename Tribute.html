Teresa Teng
One of the greatest Taiwanese singer and cultural icon

Teresa Teng

The year 2020 marks 25 years since her passing.

Let us take this time to look back on her incredible life:

  • 1953 - Born in Yunlin, Taiwan
  • 1974 - New Singer Award for the song Kūkō (空港)
  • 1979 - First Chinese singer to perform at Lincoln Centre and Los Angeles Music Center
  • 1985 - Named "Best New Singing Star" on NHK Kōhaku Uta Gassen (NHK 紅白歌合戦)
  • 1986 - Won the Japan Cable Awards (日本有線大賞) for the 3rd consecutive year
  • 1995 - Passed away in Chiang Mai, Thailand at the age of 42
  • Posthumously given state honours at her funeral
  • Awarded the Ministry of Defense's highest honor for civilians, the KMT Hua-hsia Grade One Medal and the president's commendation
  • 2009 - Voted as "The Most Influential Cultural icon in China since 1949"
  • 2010 - CNN: Top 20 biggest music icons of past 50 years
